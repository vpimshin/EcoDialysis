A. Executive Summary

  • French healthcare is a national ecological planning priority; healthcare footprint is framed as >8% of national GHG, about 50 MtCO2e, with care delivery and products/medicines both material contributors. [Gov-Health-Roadmap]
  • Dialysis is a high-resource pathway and a pragmatic entry point because it combines measurable water, energy, and consumables flows. [DR-Water][IRDES-Sustainability][SFNDT-2023]
  • Evidence-backed immediate actions: disinfection optimization, dialysis generator settings optimization, water-treatment plant optimization, centralized acid concentrate strategy, and non-clinical reject-water reuse. [DR-Water][DR-Tech][SFNDT-2026-L]
  • Reference-center assumptions retained and made transparent: 100 patients, 15,600 sessions/year, 270 L reject/session baseline, 4,200 m3/year potential stream before capture losses. [DR-France]

B. French Regulation Review (water and implementation pathway)

  • French pathway is open but conditional: sensitive healthcare settings require project-level sanitary governance and authorization logic.
  • Operational implications: separated network, sanitary logbook, prevention of interconnections, monitoring, and formal responsibilities.
  • Project discipline: classify confirmed legal position vs likely interpretation vs site-specific validation points.

Regulatory text details are summarized from official policy/legal documents and must be reconfirmed during each project filing. [Gov-Health-Roadmap]

E. Medical Devices and Consumables: Why Water Is Only the Entry Point

  • French planning and sustainability literature both indicate that products, medicines, and procurement are major contributors to healthcare climate footprint; water is necessary but insufficient as a sole lever. [Gov-Health-Roadmap][IRDES-Sustainability][HCWH]
  • Dialysis includes recurring high-volume single-use medical devices; decarbonization therefore needs procurement strategy, supplier data quality, and waste-pathway discipline. [SFNDT-2026-L][TSP-Health]
  • Scope 3 framing is mandatory for an investor-grade narrative.

J. Methodology Box - How EcoDialysis Calculates Impact

  1. Define objective and decision use.
  2. Define perimeter (clinical, technical, procurement, waste).
  3. Collect measured activity data and selected factors.
  4. Calculate baseline and intervention scenario.
  5. Run sensitivity (tariffs, capture rates, operating profiles).
  6. Run uncertainty framing and list data gaps.
  7. Publish limits and non-covered dimensions.

This follows methodological principles from the care-pathway carbon guide. [TSP-Pathway]
