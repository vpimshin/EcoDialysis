 ECODIALYSIS | 01 · The problem Every session. Every center. Every day. WATER FOOTPRINT OF HEMODIALYSIS Every 4-hour session sends ~250 L of clean water to the drain. Reverse-osmosis units reject 50–70% of the feedwater they purify · potable-grade water that never touches blood, and never leaves the hospital as anything but waste. WATER FLOW PER SESSION Mains water in 400–500 L RO unit Purifies water to dialysate standard To dialysate ~150 L ≈ 30–50% of feed RO reject → drain 250–350 L 50–70% of feedwater · potable quality · wasted FRENCH DIALYSIS POPULATION 51,700 patients on dialysis (2022) · 77% in hospital-based in-center or UDM units. NATIONAL WASTE · ESTIMATE &gt;2M m³/yr of clean RO-reject water discarded from French in-center dialysis.  ECODIALYSIS | 03 · Our solution Audit → Label → Action THE ECODIALYSIS SERVICE A three-step sustainability system built for nephrology. Starting with a transparent water &amp; energy audit, producing an energy-label-style ranking, then implementing proven RO-reject reuse · without ever touching the clinical water circuit. 01 AUDIT 1-day eco-audit, fixed price. Measures water, energy and consumables for every HD station · then translates the data into a management-ready baseline. → On-site water &amp; energy metering → Consumables &amp; waste mapping → Peer benchmark report 02 LABEL A-to-E rating, peer-benchmarked. A Best-in-class · RO reuse + optimization B Above average C National median D Improvement needed E High waste · priority action Evolves into a paid certification that centers display to regulators, patients, and partners. 03 ACT RO-reject reuse, engineered. Standardized playbook: buffer tank, UV-C sanitation, dual plumbing, backflow prevention · routed to toilets, cooling, irrigation. → Hardware kit + ARS-ready dossier → AI monitoring: anomalies, KPIs, reporting → Grant application handled end-to-end KEY PRINCIPLE Reused water never re-enters the clinical circuit. Only non-clinical uses: toilets, cooling, irrigation, external cleaning. Sources: Sustainable Healthcare Network UK · Canterbury / East Kent case guide · Malaysia MoH Sultan Abdul Halim RO-reuse report · Plos One 2022 circular-economy nephrology review · ARS EICH guidance 2024. 04 / 07
